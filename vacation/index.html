Our story

The Italian Itinerary started after a trip where we tried to do everything and came home exhausted. We went back, slowed down, and wrote better notes. Those notes turned into clear routes that keep the bright fun parts and soften the heavy parts.

Who we plan for

We help travelers who like to walk, eat well and sleep in safe, simple places. Many of our guests are on their first or second trip to Italy and want it to feel relaxed, kind and very beautiful.

Popular paths

Routes we love are Milan, Florence and Rome by train, Venice with island days, Bologna with Florence and Pisa, or a long stay in one town with easy day trips.

What you receive

You receive a day plan you can print or keep on your phone. It includes train ideas, hotel options, food stops and little reminders like where the good bathrooms are.

About me

My name is Ava Bowman and I lived in Italy for a year and a half. I spent time in Milano, Livorno, Bologna and other cities, and I fell in love with everyday Italian life.

I wanted to create the ultimate travel guide website to my favorite places to eat, see, shop and experience the Italian culture. During those 18 months I never once drove a car! I took trains, buses, bikes and walked everywhere.

I love Italy and all it has to offer, so do not wait, book the plane ticket!
