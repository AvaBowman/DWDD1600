Bologna

Bologna feels warm and rich. Covered walkways keep you shaded and the food is deep and comforting. Order anything that mentions Bolognese sauce and it will probably be good.

Food that feels like home

Adesso Pasta Car Ristorante

The lasagna Bolognese here is a favorite dish from all of Italy. Order the house special and choose a generous portion.

Via Quattro Novembre 12, 40123 Bologna

Taverna del Postiglione

Cozy restaurant with classic dishes and a nice mood. Plan ahead because you usually need a reservation.

Piada Mia

Grab and go piadina, which is a flat bread sandwich. Easy lunch when you are walking under the porticos.

Cavour Gelato

Fancy gelato spot on a busy square. It often has a line, but it is fun to stand and watch the city move around you.

Gianni Gelato
