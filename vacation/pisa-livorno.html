Pisa and Livorno

Pisa gives you the classic tower photos. Livorno adds a wide seaside walk and fresh air. Together they make an easy day or two on the coast.

Pisa

Piazza dei Miracoli

The main field where you find the Leaning Tower, cathedral and baptistery. Plan a slow walk and take your time with photos.

Open in Google Maps

Livorno

Terrazza Mascagni

Large checkerboard terrace by the sea. Good for sunset and simple photos with wide open water behind you.
