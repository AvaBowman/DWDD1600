Cinque Terre

Cinque Terre is a group of small cliffside villages by the sea. Trains connect the towns, so you can stay in one village and move between them during the day.

Plan for stairs and hills. Take breaks for focaccia, lemonade and long views of the water.

Simple ideas

Riomaggiore

Village with a small harbor and steep streets. Sunset from the rocks by the water feels very calm.

Open in Google Maps

Manarola

One of the best postcard views. Walk to the lookout point, sit on the wall and watch the colors change.

Open in Google Maps

Monterosso al Mare

The flattest village with a longer beach. Good choice if you want easy access to the water and more space.

Open in Google Maps

Use the regional train pass so you can ride between towns all day without thinking about tickets. Carry water, sunscreen and simple snacks since some paths do not have many shops.
