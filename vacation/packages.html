Vacation packages

These sample packages show how a real trip could look. Each one includes a destination focus, hotel ideas and a rental car or train plan. Prices are sample estimates for two travelers so you can see the overall scale.

Milano & Lake Day

Start with two nights in Milan so you can rest, walk the piazza and enjoy simple pizza and gelato. Then take a day trip to Lake Como for wide water views and a quiet ferry ride.

Destination: Milano with a Lake Como day trip

Hotel: Room Mate Giulia

Rental / Transport: Trenitalia regional trains, no car needed

Visit the hotel here View train schedules

Estimated package cost: $1,450 (2 travelers, 3 nights, economy flights not included)

Firenze & Tuscany Slow Days

Spend four nights in Florence with time for sandwiches, art and river walks. Add a small group bus tour to the Tuscan countryside for farm views and a simple lunch.

Destination: Firenze with a Tuscany countryside tour

Hotel: Hotel L'Orologio Firenze

Rental / Transport: Walks, local buses and a day tour coach

Visit the hotel here Sample countryside Vespa tours

Estimated package cost: $1,980 (2 travelers, 4 nights, breakfast included)

Roma, Vatican & Coast Evening

Three nights in Rome with a Vatican visit and Colosseum tour. Add one evening at the coast in Ostia or Fregene for a simple sunset walk by the sea.

Destination: Roma with a short coast escape

Hotel: Hotel Artemide, Roma

Rental / Transport: City taxis and a small rental car for the coast
