Firenze

Florence is compact and easy to walk. The Duomo, river and museums sit close together, so you can move at a slow pace and still see a lot.

These spots are simple places to eat, shop and rest while you wander the city.

Food and treats

Shake Cafe

Quick smoothies and light food near the station. Nice when you want a break from heavy meals.

Via degli Avelli 2R, 50123 Florence

Comfortable sit down pizza. Often has space without a reservation and feels friendly and calm.
