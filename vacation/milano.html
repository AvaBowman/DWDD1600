Milano

Milan is an easy first stop with clear train links and a bright main square. It feels modern and busy, but you can still find quiet corners and simple food.

These places sit near the center so you can walk between them without feeling rushed.

Places to eat

Gino Sorbillo

Pizza that feels fun and relaxed. Good for a first night when you are tired from travel.

Via Ugo Foscolo 1, 20121 Milan

Open in Google Maps

Fratelli la Bufala

Another pizza spot close to the main streets. It is a simple place with friendly staff.

Largo Corsia dei Servi 3, 20122 Milan

Open in Google Maps

Luini

Famous panzerotti to take away. The line looks long, but it moves quickly. Great for a snack near the Duomo.

Via Santa Radegonda 16, 20121 Milan

Open in Google Maps

Cioccolatitaliani

Special gelato that feels like a treat. Ask for chocolate inside the cone. It slowly melts while you walk.

Via San Raffaele 6, 20121 Milan
