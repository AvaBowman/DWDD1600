Roma

Rome is full of big sights and busy streets, but it also has quiet corners. Plan a mix of tours and slow walks so you do not feel overwhelmed.

Food and treats

Gelateria il Capriccio di Carla

Friendly gelato shop that is perfect if you are near this part of the city. Choose a cup if it is very hot so it lasts longer.

Via Agostino Depretis 61, 00184 Rome

Toto Ristorante

Restaurant near the Spanish Steps. Good for an evening meal after you have walked the main shopping streets.

Tours and sights

Night Golf Cart Tour

Many companies offer small group tours in golf carts at night. It is a fun way to see the big sights when they are lit and the air is cooler.

Open in Google Maps

Vatican Museums and St Peter's

Plan several hours here. Buy tickets ahead of time and wear comfortable shoes. The art feels endless in a good way.

Open in Google Maps

Colosseum

Look for tickets that include the higher levels or the arena floor. Being up high keeps you away from crowds and gives a full view into the structure.

Open in Google Maps

Rome Temple

Rome Italy Temple

Peaceful stop outside the city center. The visitors center is very kind and can help you call a taxi when you are ready to leave.

Open in Google Maps

It is best to take a taxi to and from the temple. Buses are possible but can feel confusing if you are new to the area.
