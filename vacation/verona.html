Verona

Verona feels gentle and romantic. The river bends around the old town and there are many bridges where you can stop and enjoy the view.

Calm places to wander

Verona Arena

Old Roman arena that now hosts concerts and events. Even when empty it feels special to sit on the stone steps.

Open in Google Maps

Ponte Pietra

Stone bridge with views up and down the river. It is a nice spot for quiet photos in the early morning or evening.
