Venezia

Venice feels like another world. There are no cars, only boats and long walks over bridges. Keep bug repellent with you since some parts of the lagoon have many mosquitoes.

Food and easy stops

Veneta Ristorazione

Calm restaurant in the San Marco area. You may need a reservation at busy times, but it is worth the effort.

San Marco 4697, Calle dei Fabbri, 30124 Venice

Open in Google Maps

l' Bacaro de' Bischeri

Small spot for quick sandwiches. It is a nice place to grab something simple while you are between bridges.

Ruga dei Oresi 57, 30125 Venezia

Open in Google Maps

Campo San Bartolomeo

Busy square near the Rialto Bridge with many little shops around it. Use it as a landmark so you do not feel lost.

San Marco 5381, 30124 Venice

Islands and music

Murano

Island known for glass work. Take a vaporetto ride and watch the factories at work. It feels slower than the center.

Murano, Venice

Open in Google Maps

Original Murano Glass Factory

This factory offers a glass blowing demo. It is interesting to watch the glass shape form in just a few minutes.

Fondamenta San Giovanni dei Battuti 4B, 30141 Murano, Venice

Open in Google Maps

Cemetery Island, San Michele

A quiet island with many graves and cypress trees. It includes the resting place of composer Igor Stravinsky.

Isola di San Michele, Venice

Open in Google Maps

San Marco Square

Classic open square where you can sit with gelato and watch the light move across the stones. Keep an eye on seagulls because they like snacks.

Piazza San Marco, 30124 Venice

Open in Google Maps

Vivaldi Four Seasons Concert

Concert where musicians play Vivaldi music in the church where he worked. Seats are simple but the sound feels special.

Chiesa della Pietà, Venice
