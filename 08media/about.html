Meet Ava

Hi, I’m Ava Bowman — the photographer behind Ava Lulu Photo. I’m based in Utah County and specialize in capturing genuine, natural moments that feel warm and timeless.

My style is a mix of soft light, clean composition, and quiet in-between moments. I want your photos to feel like you: relaxed, connected, and a little bit cinematic.

I’m also a graphic designer, which means I think about how your images work together as a story. From the way I frame a shot to the way your gallery flows, everything is considered.

What I Value

  • Comfort over posing — I’ll guide you, but I want you to move naturally.
  • Quality over quantity — thoughtful, curated galleries instead of endless duplicates.
  • Real connection — I care about who you are, not just the photos we make.

When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll probably find me designing, hunting for good light, or exploring Utah with my husband.
