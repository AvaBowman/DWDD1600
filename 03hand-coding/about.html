About

I learned photography from my mom, a retired photographer, who taught me to work simply and patiently. I shoot on a Canon Mark 7D and deliver straightforward images for web and print.

Genres

  • Portraits
  • Events
  • Product

Workflow

  1. Plan the session and shot list.
  2. Shoot with natural light or a simple two-light setup.
  3. Edit selects and export web/print files.
  4. Deliver a shared gallery.

Key Terms

Aperture
Lens opening that controls depth of field.
Shutter speed
Exposure time; affects motion blur.
ISO
Sensor sensitivity; higher values add noise.

Tools I rely on: Adobe Lightroom and Unsplash License.
