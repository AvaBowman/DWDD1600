Meet Ava

Hi, I’m Ava Bowman — the photographer behind Ava Lulu Photo! I’m based in Utah County and specialize in capturing genuine, natural moments that feel warm and timeless. My photography focuses on natural light, soft colors, and the little details that tell your story.

Whether it’s a couple sharing laughter, a family in the mountains, or an individual portrait that captures your personality, I want every image to feel personal, elegant, and alive.

I’m also a graphic designer, which means every gallery I deliver is thoughtfully composed with an eye for style and storytelling.

Fun Facts

  • I’m always chasing golden hour.
  • I love finding hidden photo spots around Utah.
  • I’m a creative through and through — from design to film.
